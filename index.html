Overview

The first hour of the 2-hour job search is spent building lists using the LAMP method. List ideal employers. Find Alumni working at these employers. Decide on Motivation to follow up at each employers. Search for Postings.

The general process should be to search for things elsewhere. For example, pick a posting or a company to follow on glassdoor, indeed, etc. Then insert postings if you can find them. Try to find contacts related to the company on linkedIn, gitHub, facebook, etc.

The goal is to get an informational interview. Add this to the calendar as an event.
